Hey! Here is a little instruction how to play. You have random weapon, that change every time when you use it. You can see his name, description, image and time in left down corner. When time will pass, you lost one life and get another weapon. You target is you beloved - triangle, she is deep underground. AD - walk, W, Space - jump. LMB - use weapon. M - turn off this awful music. Enjoy!
